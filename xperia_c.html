Xperia C5 Ultra

The big phone with a premium, slim design.

Xperia C5 Ultra’s large 6” display is the only screen you need, and the sleek and slim design is the style you want to be seen with. The super-thin bezel makes the display practically borderless and stops the phone feeling bulky. Together with a curved back and premium almuminium frame, Xperia C5 Ultra feels as good as it looks. Colour options include black, white and cool mint.

Xperia C5 Ultra may have a big display, but it’s still easy to sends texts with only one hand. Simply slide your finger diagonally to activate mini mode and shrink the screen to 4”. And when you’re ready for a big-screen experience again, just tap the mini mode away.

Colors

Three available colors.

You can choose between white, black or special green color. We think that's important for the users to have the choice. Many colors will be available in the near future and it will be possible to custom entirely the front and the back of the Xperia C5 ultra phone.

Camera

The main camera is on the front… and the back.

Now you can switch between back camera and selfie camera, without losing any of the quality. The two cameras are equal in terms of capability. Both capture stunningly crisp shots with 13 megapixels, Superior Auto and a wide-angle lens.

Selfie camera apps

Best quality for selfie

Xperia C5 Ultra’s selfie camera boasts a soft LED Selfie Flash that will create perfect shots in any light. Unlike most flashes, Xperia C5 Ultra’s flash is square instead of round, so it lights up more of the frame.

Style portrait.

Create selfies that really stand out with Style portrait. Choose from several different styles that enhance your natural look or add fun effects. Apply to photos or videos in real-time, or add after taking the shot.

AR mask.

Who will you be today? AR mask lets you place a different face over your own face or others’ faces while you snap a selfie, group photo or video. Choose from a selection of pre-loaded faces or create your own collection. Want to make your friends smile? Activate the front camera and main camera at the same time, and fuse your own face with your friends’ in real-time.

Face in.

Ever wanted to take a picture of something and capture your reaction at the same time? Face in lets you use both the front and back cameras of your Xperia device, overlaying the images in a number of unique ways.

Display

A truly big screen experience.

The generous 6” Full HD display on the Xperia C5 Ultra boasts the latest Sony TV technologies optimised for mobile. Mobile BRAVIA® Engine 2 gives you enhanced sharpness and contrast for immersive viewing. Thanks to IPS technology, this selfie camera phone brings you perfect viewing from any angle and accurate colours from edge to edge.

For an entertainment experience that’s truly beyond the ordinary, activate Super vivid mode. Colours are made more vibrant than ever, so your photos, films and videos are really eye-popping.

Processor

An Octa-core phone with lightning-fast performance.

Run multiple apps simultaneously, surf the web with shorter loading time and stream beautifully lag-free videos. The advanced 1.7 GHz Octa-core processor gives you maximum performance and speed without draining your battery. The processor is asynchronous, which means each core is powered up and down independently. So you get the precise amount of power when you need it, without wasting power when you don’t.

Sound

Crystal clear sound.

With a trio of ClearAudio+ technologies – Clear Stereo, Clear Bass and Clear Phase – your tracks are stripped of distortion and played back with perfect balance and pin-dropping clarity. And with automatic settings, all you need to do is enjoy the music.

The xLOUD loudness enhancement engine delivers your music with a deeper, richer sound and soul-shaking bass performance.

Battery

Two days of what you love.

Get directions at midnight, binge-watch your favourite TV programme or take great selfies all day long. The Xperia C5 Ultra Dual’s incredible two-day battery means you get more power from a single charge.

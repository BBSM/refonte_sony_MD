Xperia M5

Beautifully crafted for life.

Designed for beauty and durability, the Xperia M5 makes use of Sony’s classic Omnibalance™ design. With stainless steel corners and a frame that extends just a touch over the screen, it’s protected against bumps.

Colors

Three available colors.

You can choose between white, black or gold color. We think that's important for the users to have the choice. Many colors will be available in the near future and it will be possible to custom entirely the front and the back of the Xperia M5 ultra phone.

Camera

Amazing front and back camera.

Now you can switch between back camera and selfie camera, without losing any of the quality. The two cameras are equal in terms of capability. Both capture stunningly crisp shots with 13 megapixels, Superior Auto and a wide-angle lens.

Selfie camera

Best quality for selfie

Xperia C5 Ultra’s selfie camera boasts a soft LED Selfie Flash that will create perfect shots in any light. Unlike most flashes, Xperia C5 Ultra’s flash is square instead of round, so it lights up more of the frame. Our team has designed a special software to enhance and personalize your selfies

Display

Full HD experience.

The generous 5” Full HD display on the Xperia M5 Ultra boasts the latest Sony TV technologies optimised for mobile. Mobile BRAVIA® Engine 2 gives you enhanced sharpness and contrast for immersive viewing. Thanks to IPS technology, this selfie camera phone brings you perfect viewing from any angle and accurate colours from edge to edge.

For an entertainment experience that’s truly beyond the ordinary, activate Super vivid mode. Colours are made more vibrant than ever, so your photos, films and videos are really eye-popping.

Processor

64 bit Octa-core.

It delivers lightning-fast web browsing, super-fluid UIs, smooth gaming, lag-free video streaming and impressive multi-tasking. The advanced 64-bit computing runs on 8 CPU cores that power up independently so not a drop of battery is wasted.

Sound

Clear and good bass.

With a trio of ClearAudio+ technologies – Clear Stereo, Clear Bass and Clear Phase – your tracks are stripped of distortion and played back with perfect balance and pin-dropping clarity. And with automatic settings, all you need to do is enjoy the music.

The xLOUD loudness enhancement engine delivers your music with a deeper, richer sound and soul-shaking bass performance.

Waterproof

Waterproof and worry-free.

With a waterproof1 Android™ smartphone, you don’t need to worry if you get wet or dirty. Protected against both water and dust the Xperia M5 lets you carry on with your day without a worry.

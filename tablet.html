Waterproof and dust tight

The Sony Xperia Z4 Tablet has an impressive IP65/IP68 rating, which makes this HD tablet both waterproof*** and resistant to the tiniest dust particles. It can easily handle a dip in the sink. In fact, if it gets dirty you can simply rinse it off under the tap. And because the USB port is now capless, you can splash away without worrying about having to close it. You can even take pictures with it while swimming in freshwater for up to 30 minutes, at a depth of 1.5 m.

The Xperia Z4 Tablet has been given the highest IP rating in a tablet – IP65/68. This is a standard that measures a device’s resistance levels to dust (IP65) and water (IP68 – both splashes and submersion).
